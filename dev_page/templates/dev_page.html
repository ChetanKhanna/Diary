Welcome to Practice School Diaries

This website is designed for the students going for the practice school. The website will help maintain an online database that will help keep a record of the work done by these students. This database will be later made availabe to the students for future reference. Till now students used to maintain a handwritten dairy. This is the first time such an eco friendly approach has been taken and we are looking forward to its success. We have done our best to make the site as intuitive as possible, keeping in mind all the important functionalities.

The website is completely designed by the undergradguate students of BITS Pilani - Goa Campus. Please contact us if you have any suggestions.
